Hyundai

The Hyundai Elantra is a compact sedan renowned for its stylish design, efficient performance, and advanced technology. Since its debut, the Elantra has gained popularity for its value and modern features.

Key Features:
- Engine Options: 2.0L 4-cylinder, 1.6L Turbocharged
- Power: Up to 201 hp
- Fuel Efficiency: Up to 33 mpg city / 43 mpg highway
- Transmission: 6-speed manual or CVT
- Safety Features: Hyundai SmartSense, forward collision-avoidance assist, lane keeping assist
- Infotainment: 8-inch touchscreen with Apple CarPlay and Android Auto
- Comfortable and tech-savvy interior with high-quality materials

The Hyundai Elantra offers a well-rounded package with its sleek design, comprehensive technology, and fuel efficiency, making it an excellent choice for daily commuting and long drives.
