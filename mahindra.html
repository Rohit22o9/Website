Mahindra

The Mahindra XUV700 is a flagship SUV that combines rugged design, cutting-edge technology, and luxurious features. Launched in 2021, it has quickly become a popular choice in the SUV segment for its powerful performance and advanced safety features.

Key Features:
- Engine Options: 2.0L Turbocharged Petrol, 2.2L Turbocharged Diesel
- Power: Up to 200 hp
- Torque: 420 Nm
- Transmission: 6-speed manual or automatic
- Safety Features: ADAS (Advanced Driver Assistance Systems), multiple airbags, electronic stability control
- Infotainment: 10.25-inch touchscreen with Android Auto and Apple CarPlay
- Spacious and premium interior with advanced comfort features

The Mahindra XUV700 offers a perfect blend of power, comfort, and advanced technology, making it an excellent choice for both urban and off-road adventures.
