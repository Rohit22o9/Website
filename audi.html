Audi

The Audi A4 is a luxury compact sedan that combines sophisticated design with advanced technology and impressive performance. Known for its quality craftsmanship and high-end features, the A4 has been a staple in Audi's lineup for years.

Key Features:
- Engine Options: 2.0L Turbocharged 4-cylinder, 3.0L V6
- Power: Up to 261 hp
- Torque: 273 lb-ft
- Transmission: 7-speed dual-clutch automatic
- Safety Features: Audi Pre Sense, adaptive cruise control, lane departure warning
- Infotainment: 10.1-inch touchscreen with MMI Navigation Plus, Apple CarPlay, and Android Auto
- Luxurious interior with high-quality materials and advanced comfort features

The Audi A4 offers a perfect blend of performance, luxury, and technology, making it an ideal choice for those seeking a refined driving experience and a premium vehicle.
