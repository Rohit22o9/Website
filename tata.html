Tata Harrier

The Tata Harrier is a popular SUV known for its robust design, spacious interior, and advanced features. Launched in 2019, the Harrier offers a blend of performance and comfort, making it a favorite in its segment.

Key Features:
- Engine: 2.0L Turbocharged Diesel
- Power: 170 hp
- Torque: 350 Nm
- Transmission: 6-speed manual or automatic
- Safety Features: Multiple airbags, ABS with EBD, electronic stability program
- Infotainment: 8.8-inch touchscreen with Android Auto and Apple CarPlay
- Spacious and comfortable interior with premium materials

The Tata Harrier delivers a commanding road presence and offers a comprehensive set of features, making it an excellent choice for those seeking a reliable and stylish SUV.
