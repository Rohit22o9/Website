Tesla

Tesla Model S is an all-electric five-door liftback produced by Tesla, Inc. It was introduced on June 22, 2012. The Model S has an impressive range, advanced technology, and blazing acceleration, making it a top choice for electric vehicle enthusiasts.

Key Features:
- Range: Up to 396 miles (EPA estimated)
- Top Speed: 200 mph (321 km/h)
- Acceleration: 0-60 mph in 1.99 seconds
- Autopilot and Full Self-Driving capabilities
- Advanced safety features
- Spacious interior with premium finishes

The Tesla Model S offers an unparalleled driving experience, combining luxury, performance, and cutting-edge technology in one package.
