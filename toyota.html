Toyota

The Toyota Camry is a mid-size car that has been a best-seller for decades due to its reliability, comfort, and efficiency. First introduced in 1982, the Camry has evolved to offer advanced features and a smooth driving experience.

Key Features:
- Engine Options: 2.5L 4-cylinder, 3.5L V6
- Fuel Efficiency: Up to 39 mpg highway
- Top Speed: 120 mph (193 km/h)
- Safety Features: Toyota Safety Sense suite, adaptive cruise control, lane departure warning
- Comfortable and spacious interior with premium materials
- Advanced infotainment system with Apple CarPlay and Android Auto

The Toyota Camry combines practicality with advanced technology, making it a great choice for families and individuals seeking a reliable and stylish vehicle.
