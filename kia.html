Kia

The Kia Seltos is a stylish and feature-packed compact SUV that stands out for its modern design and advanced technology. Since its launch in 2019, it has become a popular choice for those seeking a versatile and comfortable vehicle.

Key Features:
- Engine Options: 2.0L 4-cylinder, 1.6L Turbocharged
- Power: Up to 175 hp
- Fuel Efficiency: Up to 31 mpg city / 41 mpg highway
- Transmission: 6-speed manual or CVT
- Safety Features: Kia Drive Wise suite, blind-spot monitoring, rear cross-traffic alert
- Infotainment: 10.25-inch touchscreen with Android Auto and Apple CarPlay
- Upscale interior with premium materials and a spacious cabin

The Kia Seltos offers a blend of style, performance, and technology, making it an excellent choice for urban driving and family adventures.
